This project analyzes unemployment trends in India with a focus on the COVID-19 period.
Key highlights:

Data preprocessing and handling missing values.

Regional unemployment analysis with bar and box plots.

COVID-19 impact analysis: pre vs. during lockdown trends.

Time series visualization of monthly unemployment rates.

Tech stack: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn.

The notebook demonstrates exploratory data analysis (EDA) techniques applied to socio-economic datasets.
